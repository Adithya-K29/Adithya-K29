## 👋 About Me

I didn’t plan to be a recruiter; I became one during COVID, by accident—and found my **purpose** in connecting brilliant engineers to teams solving real problems.  
Today I lead Talent Acquisition at Procedure Technologies, an applied‑AI engineering company that believes in one principle: “Engineers first. Technologies later.”

I’ve hired for Fortune 500 enterprises (HCLTech), agile engineering firms (Terralogic), and deep‑tech product teams (MulticoreWare: AI/ML, video codecs, compilers, HPC).  
That journey shaped my ability to build future‑ready teams across startups, scale‑ups, and high‑growth product organizations.

---

## 🚀 Key Impact & Wins

- Closed 20+ niche engineering hires across AI/ML, Cloud, HPC, and compilers.  
- Cut time‑to‑hire by 40%+ using AI tools, automation, and structured experimentation.  
- Maintained 90%+ one‑year retention through sharp screening and high‑touch candidate experience.  
- Built recruitment analytics dashboards for data‑driven hiring decisions and stakeholder visibility.  
- Helped Procedure become a “Best Place To Work” certified organization and set up Keka Hire ATS from scratch.  
- Scaled junior recruiters into top performers through coaching, playbooks, and metrics.
